# Spirit Sword

*Your mind, as sharp as a blade.*
You form a blade of mind energy and stab your target, invigorating yourself.

|                                                  |                                  |
| :----------------------------------------------- | :------------------------------- |
| **Keywords:** Animapathy, Strike, Melee, Psionic | **Type:** Action                 |
| **Distance:** Melee 2                            | **Target:** 1 creature or object |

**Power Roll + Presence:**

- **11 or lower:** 3 + P damage
- **12-16:** 6 + P damage
- **17+:** 9 + P damage

**Effect:** You gain a surge.

**Strained:** The attack deals an extra 3 damage to the target and yourself. This damage can’t be reduced in any way.
