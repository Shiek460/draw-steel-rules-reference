# Mark

You know how to focus the attention of your allies as you push them toward victory. You have the following ability:

## Mark

You draw your allies' attention to a specific foe-with devastating effect.

|                         |                        |
| :---------------------- | :--------------------- |
| **Keywords:** Ranged    | **Type:** Maneuver     |
| **Distance:** Ranged 10 | **Target:** 1 creature |

**Effect:** The target is marked by you until the end of encounter, you die, you Mark another target, or you end this effect. One Mark this way only.
While marked and line of effect(LoE), you and allies within LoE have an edge on power rolls against target.
When target reaches 0 stamina, free triggered action to Mark another target within 10 squares.

**Spend 1 Focus** for one of the following: 
- Ability deals additional damage (twice your reason score)
- Damage dealer can spend a Recovery
- Damage dealer can shift up to `your Reason score` squares.
