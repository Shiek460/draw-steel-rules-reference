# Parry

Your quick reflexes cost an enemy the precision they seek.

|                             |                            |
| :-------------------------- | :------------------------- |
| **Keywords:** Melee, Weapon | **Type:** Triggered        |
| **Distance:** Melee 2       | **Target:** Self or 1 ally |

**Trigger:** A creature deals damage to the target.

**Effect:** The attack's damage is halved. If any effect has potency, decrease potency by 1.

**Spend 1 Focus:** Target can shift a number of squares equal to your Reason score.
