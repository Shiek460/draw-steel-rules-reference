# Starting Attitudes
| Attitude | Description | Interest | Patience |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Hostile | Openly opposed to the heroes. Barely willing to listen | 1 | 2 |
| Suspicious | Doubts the heroes' motives, but willing to listen | 2 | 2 |
| Neutral | Doesn't feel one way or the other. Would rather be somewhere else, but doesn't want to be rude | 2 | 3 |
| Open | Willing to listen, willing to help, as long as the heroes aren't asking too much | 3 | 3 |
| Friendly | The heroes seem like the NPCs people. The NPC is willing to give them the benefit of the doubt. | 3 | 4 |
| Trusting | The NPC has reason to take the heroes at their word, and will help if the characters don't screw this up. | 3 | 5 |

# Stats
## Interest
- Scale of 0 - 5
- How eager they are to make a deal.
- If Interest gets to 5, they make a final offer and Negotiation ends.
- If Interest goes to 0, they end without any deal offer.
## Patience
- Scale of 0 - 5
- How much time and effort they will devote to Negotiation
- If Patience reaches 0, NPC makes final offer and Negotiation ends.
### Language and Patience
- If one hero speaks NPCs native language, not including Caelian, then patience increases by 1
## Motivations
- Each NPC has at least 2 Motivations
- Each can be appealed to only once

# Making Arguments
## Appeal to Motivation
- Can only be appealed to once per Motivation
- If Motivation is reused, Patience-1
- If same argument without a Motivation/Pitfall is used twice, automatic tier 1 result.
**Power Roll + R, I, or P**:
- <11: Patience decreases by 1
- 12-16: Interest increases by 1, Patience decreases by 1
- 17+: Interest increases by 1, Patience doesn't change
## No Motivation or Pitfall Used

**Power Roll + R, I, or P**:
- <11: Patience decreases by 1, Interest decreases by 1
- 12-16: Patience decreases by 1
- 17+: Interest increases by 1, Patience decreases by 1
- Critical: As 17+, but Patience doesn't change
## Pitfall Used
- If pitfall is used, argument automatically fails and Interest and Patience both decrease by 1. 
- May also warn PCs not to treat them in such a way again
## Caught in a Lie
- If a hero lies with an argument that ***does not*** increase Patience, NPC catches a lie and may be offended.
- Interest decreases by 1, in addition to any other decreases from the failure
