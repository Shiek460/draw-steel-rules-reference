# MALICE FEATURES
At the start of a thorn dragon’s turn, you can spend malice to activate one of the following features. Cage of Thorns ◆ 3 Malice A dragonsealed enemy within the encounter map is restrained (EoT) as a cage of thorns is grown around them. Bramble Barricade ◆ 5 Malice The dragon grows a 10 Wall of briars in unoccupied spaces on the encounter map. The wall doesn’t block line of effect for the dragon, but it does for other creatures.
