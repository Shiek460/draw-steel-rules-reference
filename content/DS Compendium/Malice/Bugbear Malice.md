BUGBEAR MALICE 
# MALICE FEATURES
At the start of any bugbear’s turn, you can spend malice to activate one of the following features. Goblin Malice Features ◆ X Malice The bugbear activates a malice feature available to goblins. Grab Iron Ball ◆ 3+ Malice For every 3 malice spent, one bugbear acting this turn grabs an iron ball. The iron ball can be thrown like a creature. A creature hit by the iron ball takes an additional 3 damage and is M<1 slowed (save ends). Grab Javelin ◆ 5+ Malice For every 5 malice spent, one bugbear acting this turn grabs a javelin. The javelin can be thrown like a creature. A creature hit by the javelin takes an additional 7 damage and is M<1 bleeding (save ends). While bleeding this way, an ally within 2 of the creature can use a free maneuver on their turn to pull the creature 2 squares. Show Them the Great Fear ◆ 10 Malice A bugbear infuses the battle map with bu’gathic magic. Until the end of the encounter, each bugbear and ally has their speed doubled, adds the Climb keyword to their speed, and inflicts I<1 frightened (save ends) on their strikes. Each affected creature then moves up to their speed.
