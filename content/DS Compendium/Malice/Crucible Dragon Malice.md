CRUCIBLE DRAGON MALICE 
# MALICE FEATURES
At the start of a crucible dragon’s turn, you can spend malice to activate one of the following features. Swordfall (While Flying) ◆ 3 Malice The dragon forms their body into a blade and drops out of the sky. Each creature under the dragon and in a 6 × 4 line within 1 takes 7 damage, A<4 4 damage for each square the dragon fell, and is restrained (save ends). On resist, the creature moves into an unoccupied adjacent square. Shower of Blades ◆ 5 Malice Each creature and object in 6 × 4 line within 1 of the dragon must make an Agility test. ✸ ≤11 16 damage; bleeding (save ends) ★ 12–16 13 damage; bleeding (EoT) ✦ 17 7 damage Meltdown ◆ 7 Malice The dragon superheats the floor until the end of the round. An enemy who starts their turn grounded is slagged (see Slag Spew).
