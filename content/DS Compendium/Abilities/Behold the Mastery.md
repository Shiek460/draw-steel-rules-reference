# ==3pt Ability== (Persist Optional)
*You open a rift to the void to harry your foes*

**Keywords**: Area, Magic, Ranged, Void

**Distance**: Cube 3 within 10

**Target**: Each enemy in the area

**Power Roll + Reason**

- **11 or lower:** 2 damage
- **12-16:** 4 damage
- **17+:** 6 damage
***Persist***: ==1pt.== At the start of your turn, you can use a maneuver to use this ability again without spending essence.
