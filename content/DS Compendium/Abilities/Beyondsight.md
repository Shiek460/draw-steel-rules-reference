# Beyondsight

You adjust your vision to allow you to see through mundane obstructions.

|                    |                    |
| :----------------- | :----------------- |
| **Keywords:**      | **Type:** Maneuver |
| **Distance:** Self | **Target:** Self   |

**Effect:** You can see through mundane obstructions that are 1 square thick or less. While your vision is adjusted this way, you can’t see and don’t have line of effect to any creatures within 1 square of you. You can return your vision to normal as a maneuver.
