WETWORK
When first held, this naginata whispers the names of its past victims

Keywords: Polearm, Psionic Item Prerequisites: A folded metal blade infused with blood Project Source: Texts or lore in Higaran Project Roll Characteristic: Might, Reason, or Intuition Project Goal: 450 

1st Level: Any damage-dealing weapon ability using this weapon deals an extra 1 rolled psychic damage. Additionally, if you kill a creature using this weapon, you can immediately use a maneuver to make a melee free strike. 5th Level: The weapon’s extra sonic damage increases to 2. Additionally, if you kill a creature using the weapon, you can use a maneuver to make a melee free strike and move up to 2 squares before or after the strike. 9th Level: The weapon’s extra sonic damage increases to 3. Additionally, if you kill a creature using the weapon, you can use a maneuver to move up to your speed and make either a signature strike or a melee free strike
