## GECKO GLOVES

  

These scaled gloves have palms and fingers covered in nearinvisible sticky hairs

  

Keywords: Hands, Magic Item Prerequisites: Ten gecko tails Project Source: Texts or lore in Caelian Project Roll Characteristic: Reason or Intuition Project Goal: 100 Effect: While you wear these gloves, your grip is all but impossible to break. You can’t be disarmed, you can’t lose your grip while climbing, and creatures grabbed by you take a bane on the test for the Escape Grab maneuver.
